About Canistro

We believe the opportunity is right away and most of the time it passes unnoticed. Canistro was created to cover this gap and uncover the good deals that are next door.

Why use canistro?

Canistro is free and open source, it is improved under your request, just let the development team know what features to add next.

Canistro is very simple to use and suitable for micro and small business, it can also be used for personal and one off selling.
